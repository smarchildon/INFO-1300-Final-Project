Entertainment

Dance performances throughout Saturday and Sunday by:

Cornell Bhangra, BreakFree, Pandora Dance Troupe, and local performers.

Kids activities include:

Fingerpainting, facepainting, henna tattoos, a merry-go-round, and other carnival games with prizes.

Great for young families!


Guest performances from:

World-renowned juggler Michael Hilby, Ira the spray-painted dog, and sword swallower Ben Rubin.
