Parking

Parking is conveniently located on Seneca Street (in between Aurora and Tioga Streets), on Green Street (just East of Cayuga Street), and on the corner of State Street and Geneva Street.

TCAT Bus Stops:

  • Corner of Aurora and Buffalo St
  • Corner of Seneca and Tioga
  • Corner of State St and Geneva St
  • On Green St just past Cayuga St.
